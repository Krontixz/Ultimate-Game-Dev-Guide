# 🧪 The Anatomy of a Command
### *Breaking down the 'Hello World'*

In this lesson, we look at the very first thing every programmer learns: making the computer speak.

---

## 📜 The 5-Year-Old Explanation
Imagine the computer is a big, friendly dog. 
* In **JavaScript**, you say: `"Computer, Bark!"`
* In **C#**, you say: `"Formal-Computer-Object, please execute the Barking-Action."`

Both get the dog to bark, but one is a bit more formal than the other!
